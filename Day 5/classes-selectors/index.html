Pedri: "My aim is to enjoy myself playing football"

Pedro González, better known as Pedri, was officially presented as an FC Barcelona player last week.. Coinciding with his presentation, BarçaTV+ premiere 'Bienvenido, Pedri', a special programme dedicated to getting to know the youngster from the Canary Islands. A Barça fan from a young age, Pedri has always stated his joy at receiving his big opportunity. In the programme he also took part in a series of games -such as trying to identify players from childhood photos. However, one of the most special moments was the surprise he received relating to his hero, Andrés Iniesta.

"I've been welcomed very well and I'm very happy. I'm really happy to have this opportunity." "Training at the Ciutat Esportiva is a luxury. I hope that I can enjoy the facilities for many years. I've been amazed." "Right away, from the moment I met my teammates, I've felt really good. Trincao and I, for example, have got on very well. Likewise, with Matheus. I am really looking forward to enjoying time with all of my teammates, and learning from them."

Barcelona reject Messi meeting request and stand firm on €700m release clause - Football Espana

Barcelona will not accept a request from Lionel Messi to hold a meeting with the club to discuss his exit and they will only sanction his exit for his €700m release clause, claims Guillem Balague. Reports in Spain have said Messi wants to meet the Barcelona board to discuss an amicable exit from the club, with the player intent on leaving this summer. The 33-year-old has a clause in his deal allowing him to leave at the end of any season, and whilst it was thought that deadline passed in June, Marca have reported his camp believe that it has been extended in line with the prolonged 2019/20 season.
